About Me

I’m Fuad Ahmed, a full stack developer with a passion for programming. Born and raised in Ethiopia, Africa, I developed a strong inclination toward technology from a young age. At 23, I have honed my skills and achieved a 3.67 CGPA during my studies at Arbaminch University, demonstrating my dedication and passion for the field.

In pursuit of greater opportunities, I made the bold decision to withdraw from my studies and move to the US. This transition has opened new doors for growth and has fueled my ambition to excel in the tech landscape.

I speak both English and Amharic, bringing a unique cultural perspective and good communication skills to any team. My diverse background and expertise make me a valuable asset in fostering an inclusive and dynamic workplace.

Beyond academics, I have honed my communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills. Serving as the president of my university’s student-run information center was a pivotal experience. It allowed me to lead, innovate, and solve complex problems while fostering a collaborative environment.
